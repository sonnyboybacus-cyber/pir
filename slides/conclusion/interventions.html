Targeted Interventions

1. Emergency Literacy

Grade 1: Daily phonics drills and "Full Refresher" pull-out sessions.

Grade 7-8: "Reading History" program to address Filipino regression.

2. SHS Enrichment

Oral Comm: Shift from theory to practice (Debates, Reporting).

Filipino: Deepen understanding of foundational linguistics.

3. Applied Science

Mandatory laboratory hours for Grade 7 (Mixtures) and Grade 11 (Mole Concept) to bridge the conceptual gap.

4. Sustain Momentum

Grade 3 & Grade 6 teachers to mentor peers on successful Reading & Science instructional strategies.
