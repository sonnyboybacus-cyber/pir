Grade 8 Achievement Rate Analysis

Comparative Performance: First Quarter vs Second Quarter

Universal Growth

Grade 8 demonstrated **consistent improvement**, with the overall achievement rate rising from ~72.8% to ~78.6%. This indicates a strong grasp of core competencies across all learning areas.

TLE Surge

TLE showed remarkable progress, jumping from 72.1% to 79.5%. This significant gain highlights the effectiveness of the hands-on, practical assessments implemented this quarter.

Approaching Excellence

Science and Val. Ed. are nearing the 80% excellence mark. This consistent high performance sets a benchmark for other subjects to emulate in the coming quarter.

The Road Ahead

With performance solidly in the high 70s, the focus shifts from remediation to **enrichment**. The goal for Q3 is to push all subjects above the 80% threshold by challenging students with advanced applications of their learning.
