Literacy Program

Reading Month Celebration 2025

"Reading is a Bridge to a Future Full of Hope"

Objectives

  • Create a safe, welcoming reading environment.
  • Engage learners in meaningful literacy activities.
  • Improve comprehension, fluency, and vocabulary.
  • Strengthen support through parent & community collaboration.

Key Activities

  • Opening Program: Inspiring messages & commitment setting.
  • Daily Literacy Boosters: Tongue twisters & vocabulary drills.
  • Share a Story: Engaging discussions on poems & stories.
  • Contests: Spelling Bee, Rebus Puzzle, Storytelling.
  • Culmination: Parade of Characters & Recognition.

Outcomes

Remarkable progress in fluency, vocabulary, and confidence across all levels. Learners showed increased enthusiasm for oral reading and storytelling, turning classrooms into lively spaces for creativity.
