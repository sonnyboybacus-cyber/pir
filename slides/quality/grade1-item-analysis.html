Mastery Gaps

The analysis reveals a significant polarization in learning. While Reading has favorable mastery levels, subjects like Makabansa and Math show concerning rates of non-mastery.

Reading Strength

Reading & Literacy emerged as the strongest domain, with 13 items mastered. This indicates that the foundational literacy programs are effective for this cohort.

Critical Areas

Makabansa (21 items Not Mastered) and Math (16 items Not Mastered) require immediate reteaching. The concepts in these subjects were clearly not grasped by the majority of the class.

Intervention Plan

Focus remediation on the 16 Math and 21 Makabansa competencies that were missed. Use visual aids and manipulatives, as Grade 1 learners may be struggling with abstract concepts.
