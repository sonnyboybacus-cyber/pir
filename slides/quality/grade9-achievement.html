Grade 9 Achievement Rate Analysis

Comparative Performance: First Quarter vs Second Quarter

High Volatility

Grade 9 performance is extremely volatile. While some subjects saw major recoveries, others experienced catastrophic drops, indicating inconsistent learner engagement or curriculum difficulty spikes.

Science Collapse

Science plummeted from a high of 93% to 39.4%, a staggering 53-point drop. English also fell significantly (-22.4%), signaling deep issues in concept retention for core subjects.

Strong Recovery

Conversely, Math (+23%) and A.P (+23.2%) showed impressive resilience, bounding back from low Q1 scores. This proves students can improve with the right interventions.

Analysis Required

Investigate the Science Q2 curriculum immediately. Was the difficulty spike planned? Adopt the remedial strategies used in Math and AP to arrest the slide in Science and English.
