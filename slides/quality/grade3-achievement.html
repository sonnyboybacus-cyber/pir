Grade 3 Achievement Rate Analysis

Comparative Performance: First Quarter vs Second Quarter

Steady Growth

Grade 3 demonstrated consistent improvement across all learning areas. The interventions applied in the First Quarter have yielded positive results, particularly in language proficiency.

English Proficiency

English leads with an achievement rate of 81.1%, making it the strongest subject. Science also showed strong performance at nearly 80%, indicating effective instruction in core content.

Focus Areas

While improving, GMRC (58%) and Math (62.2%) remain lower than other subjects. These areas require targeted reinforcement to catch up with the high language scores.

Strategy

Leverage the strong reading comprehension skills evident in English to support word problems in Math and scenario analysis in GMRC.
