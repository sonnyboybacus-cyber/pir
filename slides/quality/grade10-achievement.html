Comparative Performance: First Quarter vs Second Quarter

Mixed Performance

The Second Quarter shows a mixed landscape. While the average achievement rate adjusted to **~72.9%**, distinct bright spots emerged, countering the slight dips in Language and Math subjects.

Science Improvement

Science demonstrated excellent growth, rising to **86.00%** (from ~80%), showing that students are mastering complex concepts effectively.

EPP Excellence

EPP maintained an incredibly high standard at **96.92%**, proving consistent student engagement and mastery in practical skills.

Action Point

Focus interventions on **Math (57.5%)** and **English (63.7%)** to bring them in line with the high performance seen in Science and EPP. Peer tutoring from top performers could be an effective strategy here.
