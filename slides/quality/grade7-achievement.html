Grade 7 Achievement Rate Analysis

Comparative Performance: First Quarter vs Second Quarter

Academic Regression

Grade 7 experienced a sharp regression in core academic subjects. A.P. dropped by -30.4% and Science by -24.9%, indicating a struggle with the increased theoretical load of Junior High School.

Skills-Based Growth

In contrast, skills-focused subjects improved. MAPEH gained +16% and TLE increased by +6.4%, showing that students thrive in hands-on, practical learning environments.

Filipino Decline

Filipino also saw a significant decline of -17.7%. This widespread drop in text-heavy subjects points to a potential reading comprehension bottleneck affecting multiple disciplines.

Integration Strategy

Integrate academic concepts into MAPEH and TLE activities. Use the students' enthusiasm for practical tasks as a vehicle to teach Science and A.P. concepts.
